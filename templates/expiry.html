💡 Waste Prevention Strategies

📦 First-In-First-Out (FIFO)

Always rotate stock - move older products to the front and newer ones to the back.

🏷️ Dynamic Pricing

Offer progressive discounts: 10% off 7 days before expiry, 30% off 3 days before, 50% off last day.

🎁 Bundle Deals

Combine expiring items with fresh products as "meal kits" or "value packs".

📱 Flash Alerts

Send SMS/App notifications to loyal customers about last-minute deals on expiring items.

🔍 Batch Tracking Information

📋 How batch tracking works:
• Each purchase order creates a unique batch ID
• Batches are tracked by purchase date and expiry date
• FIFO (First-In-First-Out) is automatically recommended
• You can view detailed batch history for quality control
